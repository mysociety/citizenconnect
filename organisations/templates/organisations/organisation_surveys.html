How likely are people to recommend {{ organisation.name }}?

The Friends and Family Test (FFT) is a measure of patient experience and is used to highlight areas where action may be needed to be taken to improve quality of care.

The FFT is calculated using a simple question asked of patients based on their own experience: “How likely are you to recommend our ward/A&E department to friends and family if they needed similar care or treatment?”. Patients can then select from six responses ranging from ‘extremely likely’ through to ‘extremely unlikely’.
